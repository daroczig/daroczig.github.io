Gergely Daróczi

Former assistant professor and occasional lecturer in R, statistics and data analysis, recovering social scientist with Philosophy background – PhD in Sociology but more of a sysadmin/devops, a friend of AWS and a fan of automation, author and maintainer of a dozen of R packages and a book, passionate R developer and advocate, founder of the Hungarian R User Group and a reporting SaaS at rapporter.net, organized the first satRday and the second eRum conferences, casual data science and R freelancer/consultant, managing data operations and doing a bit of data science and engineering formerly at CARD.com and now at System1, working in Los Angeles, USA – but living in a small village of Hungary, open source enthusiast, husband, father of three.
